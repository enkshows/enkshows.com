JFK INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
To/from Manhattan: flat fare is $45.00 plus any tolls.  A NY State Tax Surcharge of $.50 will be added to each trip. If passengers request multiple stops, the flat rate of $45.00 will be collected at the first stop in either Manhattan or JFK International Airport. The metered fare should be collected at the last stop, from the last passenger.
All trips between JFK International Airport and New York City destinations other than Manhattan will use that standard metered rate.

NEWARK INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
Taxis from Newark Airport: Metered fare (plus tolls) to any Manhattan location, approximately $50-75. Taxis are available outside of every terminal and there is typically a taxi stand where you will be directed to a taxi. There is an additional charge for luggage over 24 inches if the driver handles the bags, and rates are based per car, not per passenger (up to four passengers).

LAGUARDIA AIRPORT Taxis from LaGuardia Airport: Metered fare (plus tolls) to any Manhattan location, approximately $26-30 plus tolls. Taxis are available outside of every terminal and there is typically a taxi stand where you will be directed to a taxi. There is an additional charge for luggage over 24 inches if the driver handles the bags, and rates are based per car, not per passenger (up to four passengers).

To access The Pier parking area, go up the ramp; follow the Parking signs to the roof. Park your car and take the escalator directly to street level. You will need your parking receipt when you exit. You may arrange overnight parking with the Parking Attendant upon entering the lot.
