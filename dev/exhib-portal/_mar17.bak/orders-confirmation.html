Welcome to the Show

Companies applying for participation must be approved by the Screening Committee. Companies are required to submit samples from every collection they wish to exhibit (including licensed collections), in order for the Screening Committee to determine whether the quality and design integrity of the collection meet its strict criteria.

Companies participating in other concurrent trade shows will be prohibited from participating in Coterie.

The designer’s attendance at each exhibition is strongly encouraged so (s)he can work directly with key retailers and editors.
